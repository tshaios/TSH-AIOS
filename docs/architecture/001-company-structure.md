# Purpose

This document defines the official organizational structure for TSH-AIOS Version 1.0.

It establishes the departments, reporting hierarchy, ownership, responsibilities, and communication channels used by every AI employee.

This structure is considered the authoritative organizational blueprint for all future development.

---

# Organization Philosophy

TSH-AIOS follows a hierarchical organizational model.

Every employee (Human or AI):

- Has one manager.
- Has one clearly defined responsibility.
- Owns specific business processes.
- Reports through a defined chain of command.

No AI employee reports to multiple departments.

# Company Departments

The following departments are included in Version 1.0.

1. Executive Office
2. Sales
3. Marketing
4. Engineering
5. Customer Success
6. Finance
7. Operations
8. Legal & Compliance
9. Research & Innovation

Future departments may be introduced in later versions without changing this core structure.

# Communication Rules

Departments communicate only through official workflows.

Cross-department collaboration must be coordinated by the Chief of Staff unless explicitly defined within an approved workflow.

This ensures accountability and prevents duplicated work.

---

# Organizational Principles

1. Every department has exactly one Director.

2. Every Director reports to the Chief of Staff.

3. The Chief of Staff reports to the Founder & CEO.

4. Every department owns defined business processes.

5. No responsibility may belong to more than one department.

6. Every future AI employee must belong to one department.
